Our Services

We cover every stage of visual communication — from early concept exploration to final presentation-ready imagery that drives client decisions.

01

Architectural Visualization

Exterior · Aerial

We produce photorealistic exterior renders that capture the material, scale, and context of a building before a brick is laid. From street-level perspectives to aerial overviews, our renders give developers, architects, and planners a faithful image of the final structure.

Each render is composed with an understanding of light, atmosphere, and surrounding environment — making it a powerful tool for project approvals, sales collateral, and stakeholder presentations.

Facade and elevation renders
Site and master plan visualizations
Aerial and drone perspective views
Day, dusk, and night lighting variants
02

Interior Rendering

Residential · Commercial

Our interior renders bring spaces to life with material accuracy, considered light, and a sense of atmosphere that photographs of built spaces struggle to match. We specialize in capturing the unique character of Century Ply's material range — panel textures, veneers, laminates — within the context of a fully realized interior.

Whether for a high-end residence, a corporate office, or a hospitality concept, we render spaces that communicate the designer's intent without ambiguity.

Living, dining, and bedroom renders
Commercial and retail interiors
Kitchen and bathroom detailing
Furniture and millwork close-ups
03

Product Visualization

Material · Catalogue

We render individual products and materials with studio-quality precision — from ply panels and laminates to furniture pieces and surface finishes. Our product visualization captures the tactile qualities of materials: grain, sheen, texture, and edge detail — all rendered with the same fidelity we apply to full-scale spaces.

Product renders are essential for catalogue production, e-commerce, brand materials, and specification sheets where physical samples cannot reach.

Panel and laminate swatch renders
Furniture and millwork product shots
Material exploration and finish variants
Catalogue and specification imagery
04

Animated Walkthroughs

Video · Cinematic

Our animated walkthroughs translate static renders into immersive video experiences — ideal for sales launches, presentation decks, and digital campaigns. A well-crafted walkthrough allows viewers to move through a space, sense its scale, and connect with its atmosphere in a way that static imagery cannot achieve.

We compose each animation like a cinematic sequence — with considered camera moves, lighting transitions, and spatial pacing that reflects the design's narrative.

Interior fly-through animations
Exterior and site walk-around sequences
Sales launch video production
High-resolution export for all platforms
05

Virtual Staging

Real Estate · Development

We furnish empty or incomplete spaces virtually — transforming bare photographs of rooms into fully styled, market-ready images. Virtual staging is an efficient solution for property developers and agents seeking to present units before completion, or reposition existing listings with a fresh interior concept.

The result is a photorealistic styled interior that matches the actual proportions and lighting of the physical space.

Residential unit staging
Commercial and show-home concepts
Multiple styling options per space
3-day delivery on standard projects
06

Design Development

Concept · Iteration

We work alongside architects and interior designers during the design development phase — producing iterative renders that help test material combinations, spatial arrangements, and lighting concepts before any final decision is made. It is a collaborative process built on fast feedback and clear visual communication.

Think of it as a visual design laboratory: we render your ideas quickly so you can evaluate them honestly and move toward a stronger final result.

Rapid concept renders for client review
Material and finish option testing
Spatial layout exploration
Revision rounds included

How a Project
Comes to Life

A clear, collaborative workflow from brief to final delivery — with transparency and quality at every stage.

01
Brief & Scope

We receive your drawings, references, and material specifications. A clear brief is the foundation of a precise render.

02
Modelling & Setup

Our artists build the 3D scene, assemble materials, and establish the camera angles for your review before rendering begins.

03
Render & Lighting

We apply final lighting, run high-resolution renders, and refine the image through post-processing to achieve the intended result.

04
Delivery & Revisions

Final files are delivered in your required formats. We include a round of revisions to ensure the output meets your expectations.
